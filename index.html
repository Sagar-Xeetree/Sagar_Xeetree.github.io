What is Hamro Jankari?

“Hamro janakri” is an web based application that helps to collect data in census and vital registration using computerized method. In present context data collection is done directly by going in to people’s houses in their community. Data is collected in form of physical documents in writtern form. To change this form of data collection Hamro jankari is introduced as people can give their data easily and in efficient manner. It will also be easier for data collection.
